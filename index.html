HydroToss Privacy Policy

Last modified: October 16th, 2020

Information HydroToss Collects:

HydroToss collects in-game progress of a player such as: Stars Earned, Balls Inserted, Streaks, Time Spent Playing, etc.
  • HydroToss does not require any kind of registration
  • HydroToss does not save any personal information from players
  • HydroToss is not able to identify you or anyone else by information it collects
  • We do not use information that player generates in any kind of way such as promotion, game enhancements, etc
  • Players data is saved locally on a device and is not being uploaded anywhere.


How HydroToss Uses Collected Information:

HydroToss uses collected information such as: Stars Earned, Balls Inserted, Streaks, Time Spent Playing, etc. to display progress to a player and nothing else.
